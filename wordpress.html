Everyone can have free WordPress website. This all are mine: Best world photos, Teretana Drajfus, Mlekara, Sumarak, Informalno ucenje, Claudio Caffe, Dogs, Plesna skola.
But when I was creating test sites for company Marketing Srbija I have used many themes and plugins. Themes I used are: Themify Ultra, Astra, Sydney, Bulk, Travel Agency, Online shop, WP Store, eStore. Plugins that I used are: WooCommerce, Contact Form 7, WP Google Maps, Polylang, Caldera Forms, Elementor Page Builder, Beaver Builder, Page Builder by site Origin, Site Origin Widgets Bundle, One Click Demo Import, Astra Starter Sites.


Client said that he wants site same as restaurant Zabac. I have done research and found appropriate theme for paralax site. In Themify Ultra theme, with Themify Builder, I successfully finished test site for Balkan Ekspres, and front page is almost 100% mine. I didn't use demo content for this site.


Site for forklift's parts I made in Astra Theme with eletrician demo content. For that I used Astra Starter Sites plugin.
I used Beaver Builder to modify some details of this theme and to change content with appropriate one. I used plugins for map, shop and contact form. It only took me couple of hours to finish it.
